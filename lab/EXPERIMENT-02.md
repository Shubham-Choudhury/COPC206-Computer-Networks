# EXPERIMENT - 02

## Title:

Comparison of Cable Specifications

## Aim/Objective:

To compare specifications of different networking cables.

## Theory:

Different cables have different bandwidth, speed, and performance characteristics (e.g., Cat5 vs Cat6).

## Apparatus/Equipments/Softwares:

- Internet
- Datasheets

## Procedure:

1. Search specifications of Cat5, Cat6 cables
2. Compare speed and bandwidth
3. Prepare comparison table

## Observation:

Cat6 cable provides higher speed and bandwidth than Cat5.

## Viva Questions:

1. What is bandwidth?
2. Difference between Cat5 and Cat6?
